Last updated: 12/17/2025

## Advertising
This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use information such as:
- Device identifiers (including the Apple Advertising Identifier – IDFA)
- IP address
- Information about ad interactions (impressions, clicks)
- This information is processed in an aggregated and anonymized manner and is not linked to a user’s identity by the app

This data is used to:
- Serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse
