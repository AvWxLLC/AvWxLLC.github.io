# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/17/2025

This application uses **Google AdMob** to display advertisements.

## Advertising
This app displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use information such as:
- Device identifiers (including the Apple Advertising Identifier – IDFA)
- IP address
- Information about ad interactions (impressions, clicks)
- App usage information related to ads

This data is used to:
- Serve personalized or non-personalized advertisements
- Measure ad performance
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## App Tracking Transparency
On iOS, users are asked for permission before tracking is enabled, in accordance with Apple’s App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. Users may choose whether to allow tracking.

## Data Collection by the App
This app does **not** collect personally identifiable information directly from users.
